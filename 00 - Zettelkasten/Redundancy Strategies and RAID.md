**RAID 0 - Striping**

- No redundancy, performance only
- One drive fails = all data lost

**RAID 1 - Mirroring**

- Two drives, exact copies
- One drive can fail
- 50% storage efficiency

**RAID 5 - Striping with Parity**

- Minimum 3 drives
- One drive can fail
- Good balance of performance and redundancy

**RAID 10 (1+0) - Mirrored Stripes**

- Minimum 4 drives
- Combines mirroring and striping
- High performance + high availability
